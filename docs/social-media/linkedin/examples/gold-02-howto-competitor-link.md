Your competitor already found the video topics that bring them customers.
And the list is sitting on their channel, in plain sight.

Here's how to read it in 15 minutes:

1. Open your top competitor's channel and sort by "most popular." That's real demand, not their guess at what matters.

2. Skip the brand and culture videos. Keep the ones tied to a buying decision: comparisons, "how to," pricing, use-cases.

3. Spot the pattern. If three of their top videos are "[tool] vs [tool]," that format is winning in your market. That's your signal.

4. Make your version, sharper and more specific to your buyer. Same proven demand, better answer.

This isn't copying. It's starting from what's already proven to bring buyers.
