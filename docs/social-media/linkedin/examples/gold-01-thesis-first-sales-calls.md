Your next 10 video topics are sitting in your sales calls. You're just not listening for them.

Every time a prospect asks a question on a call, that's a video. Not a maybe, a video. A buyer telling you exactly what they needed to know before they'd trust you enough to buy.

Most teams ignore that goldmine and brainstorm in a vacuum instead. Here's how to mine it, in about 30 minutes:

1. Pull your last 10 sales or demo recordings. No recordings? Start today, and have the rep jot every question for a week.

2. Write down every question the prospect asked. The objections, the "but does it..." moments, the "how do you compare to..." ones especially.

3. Group the repeats. A question in 6 of 10 calls is your next video. It's what every buyer needs answered.

4. Make one video per question, titled almost word for word how they asked it. Buyers search the way they speak.

Do this once and you've got a month of topics that map exactly to what stops people from buying. Not clever topics. The ones that close deals.
