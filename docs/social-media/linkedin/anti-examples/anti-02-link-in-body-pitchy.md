**Everything it violates:**
- No thesis; announces a topic ("competitor analysis is important")
- No value delivered, so nothing earns the click
- **Link in the BODY** → LinkedIn cuts reach 40-50% (SellonTube links belong in the first comment)
- Pitchy ("check out this tool we made," "give our tool a try") instead of teaching first
- Off-axis learn-list (topics, format, frequency, engagement) — none tied to getting customers
- Generic ("really important," "keep an eye," "an edge"), emoji + hashtag spam
- Could be posted by any account about any tool

**The contrast:** the gold version delivers a full 4-step method for free (which earns the tease), keeps the link in the comment, and anchors every step to buyer-intent topics that convert. Thesis-first forces all of that; its absence produces this ad.
