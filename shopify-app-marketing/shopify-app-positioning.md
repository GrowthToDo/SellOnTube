## The spine — four pillars mapped to the customer journey

DECIDED: these four pillars render as sections ON the `/shopify-app` landing page. No separate pillar pages for now. (See `shopify-app-architecture.md`; splitting into dedicated SEO pages is deferred.)

1. **Shopify App Store Optimization (ASO) → Discovery.** Listing copy, headlines/positioning, value props, screenshots + visual storytelling, demo & feature-walkthrough videos, use-case/outcome framing, listing conversion. Goal: a merchant instantly gets what it does, who it's for, why to install. (Mission: installs.)
2. **Website Optimization & SEO Content → Evaluation.** Landing / feature / use-case / integration / comparison pages, SEO blog, educational + problem-solution content. Goal: organic discovery, website conversion, ecosystem authority, educating before install. (Mission: installs + conversion.)
3. **YouTube & Video Marketing (for Shopify apps) → Understanding.** Demo, feature explainer, tutorial, use-case, onboarding, integration videos — reusable across listing, landing pages, sales, email, help centre, YouTube. Goal: reduce confusion, increase adoption. Scoped to this niche per the boundary — NOT the core SellOnTube service. (Mission: conversion + retention.)
4. **User Guides, Help Centres & Documentation → Success & Retention.** Help articles, user guides, onboarding docs, tutorials, FAQs, troubleshooting, knowledge base. Goal: reduce friction, faster user success, lower support burden, higher retention. (Mission: retention.) Biggest churn impact per the live page.

## The through-line

Most agencies sell isolated deliverables. We build the **complete content layer** around the product across the whole journey: Discovery → App Store, Evaluation → Website, Understanding → Video, Success/Retention → Documentation.

## ICP (unchanged — keep as-is)

A fit: technical team of any size with no one owning non-technical growth; past the first 20–100 installs with some revenue; unexplained high early churn; support inbox stealing build time; portfolio of apps or plans to build more.
