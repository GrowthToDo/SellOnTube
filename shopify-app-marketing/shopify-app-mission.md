# shopify-app-mission.md — The Mission (the filter everything answers to)

**Mission**
Drive installs, conversion, and retention for Shopify app founders — by being the growth team they don't have time to be.

We are the growth team Shopify app founders don't have. Founders build great products and run out of bandwidth before anyone hears about them. We own the non-engineering work — listing, content, documentation, reviews, pricing — so installs, conversion, and retention all climb. Their growth is the mission; our leads are the byproduct of delivering it.

**How to use this file:** Every page, tool, resource, and line of copy must serve one of three client outcomes — **installs, conversion, retention.** If a thing doesn't map to one of those, it does not ship. Leads are never the stated goal in any public-facing content; they are what delivering the mission produces.
