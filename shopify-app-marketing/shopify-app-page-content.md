## Problem section — "Good apps stay invisible. Not because they're bad."
01 Your listing loses merchants before they click — weak title, vague description, no reviews; merchants scroll past in under three seconds. The listing is the first sale.
02 Merchants uninstall in week one — high early churn is almost always an onboarding/documentation problem.
03 You answer the same support questions daily — without a knowledge base you become the documentation.
04 Reviews pile up with no response — unanswered reviews signal nobody's minding the store; velocity affects ranking.
05 No content working while you ship — user guides, SEO, video compound; most apps skip them.
06 You are the engineer and the entire company — the non-technical work always gets dropped.

## Fit section — "You are a fit if your app looks like this."
Solo technical founder / small eng team, no non-technical staff · active installs + some revenue, past first 20–100 · high early churn you can't explain · support inbox stealing build time · multiple apps or plans to build more.
Not a fit: <20 installs · teams already running in-house marketing · founders wanting purely paid-ads management.
