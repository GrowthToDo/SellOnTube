## Summary

- 296 links proposed across 8 batches (blog x4, youtube-for, youtube-vs, hub, tools).
- 293/296 passed deterministic verification (target validity, redirect safety, banned words, verbatim insertion-point check); 3 dropped for unverifiable quote match.
- A cross-batch anchor-diversity aggregation pass found and fixed 18 violations before apply (identical anchor phrase reused >3x for one target within the new-proposals set).
- A post-commit review found the diversity check had not been cross-referenced against the *live* site corpus, missing 29 additional groups where new links combined with already-published anchors pushed a (target, anchor) pair over the 3-source cap. All 47 individual new-link occurrences responsible were rewritten to distinct natural anchor variants (pre-existing, already-published anchor text was never touched).
- Final: 0 in-scope orphans, 0 dead-ends, 0 pages missing a tool link, click-depth->3 pages reduced 129 to 92.
