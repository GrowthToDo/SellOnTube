**CONTENT ANGLE (NON-OBVIOUS INSIGHT):**
Shorts are a repurposing channel, not a content strategy. For B2B, Shorts work when they are clips extracted from long-form videos that already target search intent. A B2B company with zero long-form videos should not start with Shorts. A B2B company with 20 solid long-form videos is sitting on a goldmine of Shorts clips.

Second insight: Shorts viewers and long-form viewers are different audiences on the same platform. YouTube's algorithm treats them as separate feeds. A Short going viral will not drive subscribers who watch your 15-minute videos. It builds awareness, not lead gen.
