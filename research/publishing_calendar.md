Max posts in any 7-day window: **4** (including existing scheduled posts).

- Each post targets a distinct keyword cluster (no cannibalization)
- All are 1000-1500 word unique content (not templated)
- pSEO publishing is paused (net content velocity stays similar)
- Posts with CPC $30-90 prove Google Ads advertisers value these queries
