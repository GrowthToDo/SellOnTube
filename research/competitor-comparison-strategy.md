## Positioning Angle

SellonTube is NOT a creator tool. VidIQ/TubeBuddy optimize for views and subscribers. SellonTube optimizes for leads, pipeline, and ROI. The comparison articles don't compete feature-for-feature -- they reframe the category: "You're not looking for a better VidIQ. You're looking for a different kind of tool entirely."

## Content Guidelines

1. **Never fake a feature comparison.** SellonTube doesn't have the same features as VidIQ/TubeBuddy. The article reframes the question, not the answer.
2. **Lead with the buyer's actual problem.** "You searched for VidIQ alternatives because [pain]. Here's why that pain exists -- you're using a creator tool for a business problem."
3. **Be honest about what SellonTube is and isn't.** Credibility > hard sell.
4. **Internal link to relevant tools** (YouTube SEO tool, ROI calculator, tag generator) as proof points.
5. **Follow blog cadence rules** -- 1/week max, check publishDate collisions before scheduling.
