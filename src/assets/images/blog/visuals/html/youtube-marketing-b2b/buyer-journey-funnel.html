Problem-Aware Content
Top of Funnel
Reaches buyers who know something is wrong but haven't defined the solution. No pitch — just direct value. Builds the widest audience.
"Why is my SaaS churn so high?" "How to stop losing deals at demo"

Solution-Aware Content
Middle of Funnel
Reaches buyers evaluating options. Shows your methodology and point of view. This is where trust is built — publish 2× more of this than any other type.

Proof Content
Bottom of Funnel
